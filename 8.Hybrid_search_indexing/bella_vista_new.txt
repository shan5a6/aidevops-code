Bella Vista is open from 11 a.m. to 11 p.m. from Monday to Saturday.
On Sundays, we welcome guests from 12 p.m. to 10 p.m.
Bella Vista offers Mediterranean and contemporary American cuisine.
We offer vegetarian and vegan dishes, and our chefs can customize meals.
Bella Vista is family-friendly and has a kids' menu.
Private events can be booked with us, and we offer catering services.
The ambiance is cozy and elegant with a view of the city skyline.
Reservations are recommended on weekends and holidays.
